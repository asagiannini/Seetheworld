Always Being Right

Always being right is the belief that it is entirely unacceptable to be wrong. Even if you have to hurt other people, ignore your own failings, and stretch the truth, you must always be right. Your desire to be right about what you’re doing has gone to such an extreme that you are frightened of even the possibility of being in the wrong.

Challenge your need to be right. Remind yourself that no one is right all the time, and it’s ok to be wrong. Remind yourself that you have been wrong before and will be again, and there’s nothing wrong with admitting it.

When you feel that you have to be right, stop and admit to yourself that possibly or even definitely, you are in the wrong. Think to yourself about how it’s more important to treat yourself and others well that it is to always be right.
