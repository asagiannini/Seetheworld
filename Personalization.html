Personalization

Personalization is the feeling that the negative things that happen around you are somehow your fault no matter how unlikely it may logically seem. Itâ€™s feeling important in the worst possible way as if you were responsible for things that you could not or did not cause.

Challenge your personalizing thought. Remind yourself how even if you feel like bad things around you are your fault, they are not. You are not responsible for the things that go on around you even if it can feel intuitively like you are.

Formulate a framework for thinking about the events in your life. Learn to say to yourself when you feel guilty or scared that it is illogical and unreal to blame yourself for the bad things that happen around you.
