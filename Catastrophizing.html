Catastrophizing

Catastrophizing is believing that small negative instances are in fact disasters that you cannot recover from. You may feel that a slight mistake means the end of your job or a slight failure means youâ€™ve failed completely. Meanwhile, you will likely feel that even the most positive of events is entirely minor and unimportant.

Challenge your catastrophic thoughts. Take each instance as a small part of your life and remember that when you feel scared and think a disaster has occurred or will, this is not the case. Your life is made up of many instances and you have not failed.

When you feel yourself panicking or feeling like the events around you are horrible, stop and tell yourself that no matter what you feel, the instances are actually trivial. Even if things feel very bad, they are only minor setbacks.
