Filtering

Filtering is fixating on the things in your life that are bad while ignoring the things that are good. Anything that could cheer you up is filtered out, leaving only the things that make you sad or upset. Do you find yourself focusing on things that make you unhappy and ignoring things that don’t?

Challenge your filtering thought. Remember and say to yourself that there are good things in your life and it’s not realistic to focus on just the negative. Your view of the world as being all bad is unrealistic and unhelpful: a distortion of the truth.

Formulate a positive believe and put it into action. Come up with a sentiment that demonstrates how you might positively think about your situation. Make it concrete and specific. When you are tempted to feel sad or upset, remember this positive belief and make it the first thing you think of.
