Fallacy of Fairness

The Fallacy of Fairness is unhealthy fixation on whether things are fair or not. While there is nothing wrong with seeking or believing in fairness, the inability to move past instances that are not fair can lead to a sad or cynical view of the world. Constantly viewing every circumstance in terms of fair or unfair will lead to an inability to move past circumstances that you have to deal with.

Challenge your fixations on fairness. Understand that whether a situation is fair or not, it has to be dealt with. Tell yourself that you have to move on from questions of fairness in order to keep living your life.
