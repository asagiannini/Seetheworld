Control Fallacies

Control fallacies are the mistaken feelings that we are either completely in control or completely out of control of your life. Each even that happens to you must either be your fault or the fault of external forces you can’t control. You may feel that bad things that happen are either your fault or the fault of someone else without stopping to consider that you can’t know and it may be neither.

Challenge your fallacies of control. Realize that you can’t always know who is in control and that you are definitely not always to blame. Remember that the world is complicated and while things are sometimes beyond your control that doesn’t mean everything that happens to you is someone else’s fault.

When you feel yourself beginning to ascribe event in your life to yours or other’s control, stop and tell yourself that you don’t necessarily know, and it doesn’t matter who is in control. Verbalize this thought and try to move forwards without being bothered about controlling or being controlled.
